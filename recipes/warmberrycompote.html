Warm Berry Compote

Indulge in a delightful, simple, and comforting warm berry compote, perfect for topping pancakes, waffles, or ice cream.

This berry compote is made in a slow cooker with frozen berries and orange juice. It's a yummy dessert by itself or over ice cream.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of mixed berries (fresh or frozen)
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, combine berries, sugar, and lemon juice.
  2. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the berries break down and the mixture thickens, about 10 minutes.
  3. Stir in vanilla extract.
  4. Serve warm over your favorite desserts or breakfast treats.
